Privacy Policy

Effective date: 09 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Snopy ("we", "us", "our") processes personal data when you use the Snopy mobile application (the "App").

1) Scope

This policy applies to the App and related support communications. It does not apply to third-party websites or services that have their own policies.

2) Data We Process

  • Device and technical data: device model, OS version, app version, language, and basic diagnostics.
  • Usage data: interactions needed to operate features and improve app stability.
  • Advertising data: identifiers and signals used by ad partners to deliver, limit, and measure ads.
  • Purchase/subscription data: product identifiers, entitlement status, and transaction metadata needed to validate access.
  • Support data: information you choose to share when contacting us (for example by email).

3) How We Use Data

  • To provide and maintain App functionality.
  • To secure the App, detect abuse, and fix issues.
  • To process subscriptions and restore purchases.
  • To show ads in the free experience and measure ad performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

4) Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

  • Contract: processing required to provide requested App features.
  • Legitimate interests: app security, troubleshooting, and service improvement.
  • Consent: where required, including consent choices gathered via Google UMP and tracking choices under ATT.
  • Legal obligation: compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

5) Advertising, ATT, and Consent

The App uses advertising technology including Google AdMob. Depending on your region and settings, we may request consent for personalized advertising and tracking.

  • ATT (Apple App Tracking Transparency): iOS permission prompt may be shown before tracking-enabled advertising.
  • UMP (User Messaging Platform): consent messages may be shown in regions where consent is required (including EEA/UK as applicable).
  • You can update device-level tracking settings in iOS at any time.

6) Third-Party Processors and Services

We may use third-party providers to deliver parts of the service. These providers may process data under their own privacy terms.

  • Google AdMob (ads delivery and measurement)
  • Apple (in-app purchases, subscriptions, and App Store billing)
  • Subscription/purchase infrastructure used by the App

7) Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with service providers to operate the App, with legal authorities when required by law, or during business transfers (for example merger/acquisition), subject to safeguards.

8) Data Retention

We keep data only for as long as needed for the purposes described above, including legal, accounting, fraud-prevention, and dispute-resolution needs. Retention periods may vary by data type and provider.

9) International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers.

10) Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as access, correction, deletion, objection, restriction, portability, and withdrawal of consent. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

11) Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age where parental consent is required by applicable law. If you believe a child provided personal data without permission, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

12) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

13) Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above indicates the latest revision date.
